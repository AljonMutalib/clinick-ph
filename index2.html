About Clinick PH

Clinick PH was born out of a desire to modernize the healthcare experience in Zamboanga City. We noticed the struggle of local families waiting hours in clinic lobbies and the administrative burden on our hardworking medical secretaries.

Our Mission

To provide every Zamboangueño with seamless, digital access to local healthcare services, reducing stress and improving health outcomes.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital healthcare bridge in the Zamboanga Peninsula, connecting patients and doctors through reliability and care.
