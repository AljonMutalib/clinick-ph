About Clinick PH

Clinick PH is a digital health platform dedicated to improving the medical consultation experience in Zamboanga City. We bridge the gap between clinics and patients through seamless technology.

Our team is committed to reducing wait times and making healthcare administration more efficient for both doctors and families.

For Medical Clinics

Privacy Policy

At Clinick PH, we prioritize the security and privacy of your health data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Data Collection

We collect personal information such as name, contact details, and appointment history only to facilitate medical services.

Compliance

We adhere to the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) to ensure your information is protected at all times.

Terms of Service

By using Clinick PH, you agree to comply with our usage terms designed to maintain a fair and efficient scheduling system for everyone.

User Conduct

Users are expected to honor booked appointments or cancel with sufficient notice to allow other patients to take the slot.

Service Limitation

Clinick PH is a scheduling platform and does not provide medical emergency services. In case of emergency, please visit the nearest hospital.
