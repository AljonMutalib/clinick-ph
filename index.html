Our Mission

Better clinic visits for Zamboanga.

Clinick PH is a homegrown digital project starting right here in Zamboanga City. We noticed how much time families spend in waiting rooms, so we decided to build a simple way to bridge that gap.

We aren't trying to replace the personal touch of your local doctor; we're just here to handle the paperwork and the queues, making the admin side of healthcare a little quieter and more organized.

Focus on care, let us handle the coordination.

Clinick PH is designed to fit into your existing clinic routine. We provide simple digital tools to help your staff manage the daily flow of patients more smoothly.

Organized Waiting Lines

A basic digital queue that lets patients see their status from their phones. It helps reduce crowded waiting rooms and keeps your front desk from being overwhelmed with "How much longer?" inquiries.

Simplified Logging

Keep a clear, digital record of patient visits. It’s not about replacing your paper files entirely—it’s about having a searchable, secure backup that stays organized for you.

Currently in local pilot testing. We’re working closely with Zamboanga clinics to refine these tools.

Privacy Policy

How we handle your data with care.

Last Updated: March 2026

Our Commitment

At Clinick PH, we believe your health data belongs to you. As a new platform, we are committed to building trust by being transparent about what we collect and why we collect it.

What We Collect

To help you book appointments, we only ask for the essentials:

  • Full Name
  • Contact Number
  • Appointment History
  • Basic Profile Details

Legal Compliance

We fully adhere to the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). Your information is encrypted and stored securely, and we never sell your personal data to third parties. Our goal is to keep your clinic visits private and your data protected.

Terms of Service

Simple rules for a better clinic experience.

Not for Emergencies

Clinick PH is a scheduling tool, not a medical provider. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please proceed immediately to the nearest hospital or ER in Zamboanga City.

Fair Scheduling

To keep the system fair for all Zamboangueños, we ask that you only book slots you intend to keep. If you can't make it, please cancel as early as possible so another patient can see the doctor.

Our Role

We provide the platform to connect you with clinics. While we strive for accuracy, the final schedule and medical advice are always managed by your healthcare provider. We are here to make the coordination easier, but the clinic's own rules still apply.

Account Responsibility

Please keep your login details safe. Since Clinick PH is still in its early stages, we may occasionally update features or terms to better serve our community.

By using our platform, you agree to these simple terms. Thank you for helping us build a better healthcare experience.
